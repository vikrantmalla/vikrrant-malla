Overview

About Project

Finsta World is an online social platform where people can easily find companions that share similar interests and be able to meet with them to take part in activities of their liking.It is a platform where people can easily find companions that share similar interests and be able to meet with them to take part in activities of their liking.

Challenges

The biggest challenge was to create a unique application which can provide the latest news about any celebrity or friend from any social networking platform in one place.

Perfection in the sense of UI (user interface) with smooth animations throughout was the last challenge for us.

It was quite challenging to deploy some unique, useful and advanced features in the application which helps in providing more engagement to its users.

Responsibilities

Qualitative Research, UX Design, Visual Design, Prototyping
